Enrollment & Access

To enroll, simply click the "Join Now" or "Enroll Now" button on any course page. You'll be prompted to create an account or log in, after which you can complete the enrollment process.

Yes! Once you purchase or enroll in a course, you have permanent access to all materials, including future updates, as long as your account is active.

Payments & Refunds

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay/Google Pay for your convenience.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our professional programs and individual courses. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase.

Certificates

Absolutely. Upon successful completion of all course modules and assessments, you will be issued a digital Certificate of Mastery that you can share on LinkedIn or include in your portfolio.

Our certificates are highly respected and recognized by top technology companies and design firms because they represent the completion of practical, industry-aligned curricula.

Technical Issues

All you need is a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and a stable internet connection. Our platform is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

First, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If the issue persists, please clear your browser cache or try a different browser. Still stuck? Contact support@smartlearn.com.
