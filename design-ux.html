Design & UX Systems

Elevate your creative skills beyond aesthetics. Master a systematic approach to product design and user experience that drives real results.

10 Weeks
Professional Portfolio

Program Overview

This program bridges the gap between creative visual design and data-informed user experience. You'll learn how to build scalable design systems, conduct thorough user research, and prototype complex interactions that solve user problems while meeting business objectives.

What You'll Learn

  • User Research Methodologies
  • Architecting Design Systems
  • Advanced Figma Workflows
  • Prototyping & Micro-interactions
  • Accessibility Standards (WCAG)
  • Stakeholder Mock Pitching

Key Outcomes

Industry-Ready Portfolio

Complete multiple end-to-end UX case studies that demonstrate your problem-solving process to employers.

Systems Thinking Mastery

Understand how design translates into development and how to maintain consistency across large platforms.
